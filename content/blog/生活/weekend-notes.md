周末总是过得特别快。

## 周六早晨

 woke up naturally at 9 AM, made a cup of pour-over coffee. The sun was shining through the window, everything felt calm and peaceful.

## 下午阅读

Spent the afternoon reading a novel in the park. The breeze was gentle, and the sound of children playing nearby added to the atmosphere.

## 周日

Just a lazy day at home, tidying up and preparing for the week ahead.

Sometimes the simplest weekends are the best ones.
